This coaching is for you if

You already know you are capable of more. You want to stop shrinking yourself to fit someone else’s idea of professional.

You feel stuck and underpaid

  • You are overqualified and under recognised at work.
  • You want a role that fits how you actually think and work.
  • You are ready to move, even if you are not yet sure where.

You want to ask for more

  • You want a raise, promotion or better conditions.
  • You do not know how to frame your case without feeling needy.
  • You want a strategy that respects your identity and context.

You are tired of navigating it alone

  • You know bias and power are in the room, even when no one says it.
  • You want a coach who names it and works with it, not around it.
  • You want clear moves, not vague motivation quotes.

How this is different

We combine CliftonStrengths tools with DEI practice and social justice thinking. The goal is not to fix you but to shift your position and choices.

Strengths first

Strengths, not flaws

We start from your CliftonStrengths profile and map where you are already powerful. You get language for how you think, feel and work so you can stop apologising for it.

Power aware

Systems, not just mindset

We talk honestly about bias, politics and power, so you can pick strategies that keep you safe and effective in real organisations, not imaginary fair spaces.

Action focused

Action, not endless reflection

Every session ends with one clear move. A conversation, an email, a boundary, a decision. Small steps that build momentum instead of more overthinking.
