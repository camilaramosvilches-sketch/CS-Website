Work with me

All offers are built around CliftonStrengths, a social justice lens and clear action in your real context. Sessions are online and in English or Spanish.

Pivot plan

Pivot Plan

For people ready to move into a different role or field.

  • 4 sessions over 6 to 8 weeks.
  • Includes CliftonStrengths assessment if you do not have it yet.
  • Email support between sessions for questions and small wins.

We map your strengths, values and limits, and we design a pivot that is realistic for your life. You leave with a clear direction, a narrative for your CV and LinkedIn, and concrete next steps.

Ask for more

Ask for more

For people who want a raise, promotion or better conditions.

  • 3 sessions over 4 to 6 weeks.
  • Evidence and story work based on your strengths and impact.
  • Scripts and practice for the actual conversation.

We identify how your strengths already add value where you work. We gather evidence, map power, and script the conversation. You leave with a negotiation plan, clear wording and a support strategy if the answer is no.

Power in place

Power in place

For people who cannot or do not want to leave yet.

  • Ongoing one to one coaching, once or twice per month.
  • Focus on boundaries, visibility and influence in your current role.
  • Session by session plan that adapts to what is happening at work.

We use your strengths to decide where to put energy and where to stop over giving. You leave with more clarity, more choice and more tools to navigate your current context.
