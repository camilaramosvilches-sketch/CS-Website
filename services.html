Coaching Services

I offer a range of coaching experiences to meet you where you are. All sessions can be delivered virtually or in person and include follow‑up resources tailored to your needs.

Discovery Session

An introductory one‑hour session to review your CliftonStrengths results and explore how your top themes show up in your life. Great for those new to the assessment or looking for a refresher.

€100 / 1 hour

Strengths Deep Dive

A series of five one‑hour coaching sessions scheduled over 6–8 weeks. We’ll unpack all 34 themes in your profile, identify areas for growth and create a personalized action plan to leverage your strengths at work and beyond.

€450 / 5 hours

Team Workshop

A facilitated three‑hour workshop for teams (up to 10 participants). Each member receives their CliftonStrengths report, and together we explore the team’s collective talent map, highlighting complementary partnerships and strategies for collaboration.

€750 / 3 hours

Ongoing Coaching Package

Designed for individuals or leaders seeking long‑term support. Includes ten one‑hour sessions across several months, unlimited email support and periodic check‑ins to sustain momentum and accountability.

€900 / 10 hours
